About Us

Welcome to The Lighthouse, your one-stop destination for Christian gifts! Established on 1 December 2023, in the picturesque town of Dullstroom, Mpumalanga, our store is dedicated to providing a unique selection of Christian gifts for both adults and children.

At The Lighthouse, we believe in spreading positivity, inspiration, and the joy of faith through thoughtful and meaningful gifts. Our curated collection includes a wide range of products that reflect the essence of Christian values.

Where to find us

Visit us in the heart of Dullstroom on the main road, just to the left inside the Dullstroom Waffle House. Our location offers a welcoming atmosphere where you can explore our carefully chosen Christian gifts. Whether you're looking for something special for a friend, family member, or yourself, The Lighthouse is the place to find meaningful and inspiring items that resonate with your faith.
