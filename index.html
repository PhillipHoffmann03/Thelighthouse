Welcome to The Lighthouse

Your one-stop destination for Christian gifts

Established on 1 December 2023 in Dullstroom, Mpumalanga, The Lighthouse offers a thoughtfully curated collection of Christian gifts for all ages. From spiritual keepsakes to uplifting decor, we aim to inspire and encourage through meaningful items that reflect faith and love.

About Dullstroom

Dullstroom is a charming village known for its cool climate, scenic beauty, and tranquil surroundings. It’s a popular destination for fly-fishing, art lovers, nature seekers, and those looking for a peaceful escape in the mountains. With quaint restaurants and unique boutiques, it’s the perfect getaway location.

Where to find us

You’ll find us on the main road of Dullstroom, just to the left inside the Dullstroom Waffle House. Stop by to explore our Christian gift shop in a peaceful, inviting space filled with inspiration for everyone.
